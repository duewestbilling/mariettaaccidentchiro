Common Injuries We Treat

What Happens to Your Body in a Crash

Many accident victims feel fine immediately after a crash — only to wake up days later in serious pain. Adrenaline masks pain, and soft tissue injuries take time to fully manifest. Don't wait to find out.

⚠️ Don't Wait to Be Seen

Documenting your injuries starting from day one protects both your health and your legal claim. Insurance companies use gaps in treatment to minimize payouts. Call us the same day or the morning after your accident — same-day appointments are often available.

🔄

Whiplash

The most common crash injury. Cervical strain from rapid head movement causes lasting pain if untreated.

💢

Herniated Discs

Impact force can rupture spinal discs, causing nerve compression, shooting pain, and numbness.

🦴

Soft Tissue Damage

Muscle tears, ligament sprains, and connective tissue injuries that can take months to heal without treatment.

⚡

Nerve Compression

Pinched nerves from spinal displacement cause radiating pain, tingling, and weakness in arms or legs.

🤕

Headaches & Concussion

Post-accident headaches, dizziness, and cognitive symptoms that require prompt evaluation and documentation.

🚑

Multi-Trauma Injuries

From shoulder and knee injuries to complex spinal trauma from high-speed or commercial vehicle collisions.
